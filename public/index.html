✦ Core Features

Tools for Balanced Screen Time

Everything parents need to guide healthy digital habits — built with simplicity and transparency in mind.

⏰

Screen Time Limits

Set daily time budgets and device schedules to help children maintain balanced screen usage. Customize limits by day of the week — stricter on school days, flexible on weekends.

📅
🚫

App Management

Allow, block, or limit specific apps based on your family's guidelines. Full control stays with you as the parent.

🗂️

Category-Based Controls

Organize apps into categories like Games, Social, or Education and apply time limits to whole groups at once — no need to manage every app individually.

🏷️

Custom App Categories

Create and manage your own categories to organize apps in ways that match your family's unique rules and routines.

🔒

Restricted Categories

Mark categories like gambling or adult-oriented apps as restricted. Block every app in those groups with a single toggle whenever needed.

📖

Educational App Info

For certain apps, view clear explanations of potential risks for children and guidance on how to approach safe usage conversations.

📊

Usage Insights

Detailed reports show how devices are used across apps and categories, helping families understand habits over time — not just today.

🌙

Bedtime & Focus Schedules

Automatically restrict devices during bedtime, homework, or family time. Build healthy routines with consistent, automatic boundaries.
