📱 Section 1

What This App Does

A clear picture of Match's purpose, history and the adult audience it is built entirely for.

📅
One of the World's Oldest Dating Platforms
Match has operated as a paid adult dating service since 1995 — making it one of the longest-running online dating platforms in existence. Its entire infrastructure, culture and user base has always been adult-focused and relationship-oriented.
Match is a subscription-based online dating platform that connects adults seeking romantic relationships. Users build detailed profiles, browse potential matches and communicate through messaging — all within an environment designed exclusively for adult romantic life.
💘
Profile-Based Relationship Matching
Users create detailed profiles covering personality, lifestyle, relationship goals and physical preferences. Match surfaces compatible profiles based on these inputs — all centred on adult romantic intent.
💬
Private Messaging & Winking
Users can message matches directly or send a "wink" to express interest. Private messaging between adult strangers is unmoderated and can include mature conversations and personal disclosures.
📍
Location-Based Discovery
Match surfaces profiles based on geographic proximity, revealing a user's approximate location to adult strangers. Children using the platform would inadvertently share location data in this way.
📸
Photo-Led Profile Discovery
Profile photos are central to how users are surfaced and evaluated. Physical appearance-based selection shapes social expectations in ways that can negatively affect developing adolescents.
🔔
Daily Match Suggestions
Match actively sends daily profile suggestions to keep users engaged. This push-based design creates habitual return visits and sustained engagement with adult dating content.
💳
Subscription-Based Access Model
Full messaging access requires a paid subscription. Children gaining access may use a parent's payment method, creating both financial and safety concerns for families.
🔞
Match Requires Users to Be 18 or Older The platform has been built for adult romantic relationships since 1995. Every feature, profile type and community norm reflects that — there is no child-safe mode or restricted content layer.
⚠️ Section 2

Why It May Not Be Appropriate for Children

Key risks that arise when children access platforms built entirely around adult relationships.

💑
Adult Relationship Focus
Match exists entirely for adult romantic connection — a context wholly inappropriate for children at any age
💬
Mature Conversations
Private messaging between strangers is unmoderated and may contain adult, intimate or explicit content
👤
Contact With Unknown Adults
Children can match and message with unverified adults who have no awareness of the child's real age
📍
Location Data Exposure
Location-based profile surfacing reveals a child's approximate whereabouts to adult strangers on the platform
💳
Financial Risk
Full access requires a paid subscription — children may use a parent's saved payment method without their knowledge
🪞
Self-Image Pressures
Appearance-led profile evaluation can affect a child's developing sense of worth and body image significantly
💳
Subscription Model Creates an Additional Financial Risk
Unlike free dating apps, Match charges a monthly subscription fee to unlock full messaging. Children with access to a parent's device or saved payment method could make purchases without parental knowledge. Review linked payment accounts regularly.
👨‍👩‍👧 Section 3

What Parents Should Know

1
Dating Apps Are Designed for Adults 18 and Above
Match's three decades of operation have always been centred on adult romantic relationships. Its content, community standards and feature set have never been designed with younger users in mind, and the platform offers no child-appropriate mode.
2
Curiosity and Peer Influence Are the Main Entry Points
Children and teenagers may download Match out of curiosity or because older peers or siblings use it. This is natural — but accessing a paid adult dating service exposes them to unmoderated adult content and strangers without any protective safeguards.
3
Age Verification Can Be Easily Bypassed
Match requires a date of birth and email address to sign up. A child can enter a false birthdate and create a full account in minutes. With a paid subscription available via stored payment methods, the access path is simpler than many parents realise.
4
Build Foundations Before They're Needed
The most protective thing a parent can offer is a strong foundation — open conversations about relationships, healthy communication, online safety and financial awareness. These conversations are most effective when they happen before a child encounters adult platforms, not after.

💡 Section 6

Guidance for Parents

Three practical steps to protect your child and build lasting digital resilience.

💬
Discuss Online Relationships and Digital Safety
Have open, age-appropriate conversations with your child about how online dating works, why it exists for adults, and what healthy, respectful relationships look like in real life. Children who feel comfortable discussing relationships with a parent are far better protected from unsafe online interactions.
🤝
Encourage Communication Within Trusted Social Circles
Help children prioritise and invest in the friendships and family relationships they already have, rather than seeking connection with unknown adults online. Strong, trusted real-world relationships are the most effective antidote to the appeal of anonymous platforms.
🔒
Use Parental Controls to Prevent Access to Adult-Focused Apps
Apply iLimits restrictions consistently across every device your child uses. Revisit your settings regularly as they grow and their digital access evolves. Also review saved payment methods on shared devices to prevent unauthorised subscription purchases. Controls and conversations, used together, are your most powerful tools.

Children need time, space and guidance to understand what healthy relationships look like before they encounter the world of adult dating. iLimits helps you hold that space — so your child can grow into their own story with confidence, safety and your support.
