A clear picture of Mamba's CIS-focused dating platform, features and the adult community it is built for.

Mamba Is One of the Largest Dating Platforms in Russia and the CIS Region
Launched in Russia and widely used across Russia, Ukraine, Belarus and neighbouring countries, Mamba has tens of millions of registered users and is one of the most downloaded dating apps in the CIS region. Its combination of location-based discovery, video features and a virtual economy makes it significantly more feature-rich than many comparable platforms — and therefore carries a broader range of risks for children who access it.

Mamba's radar feature surfaces other users ordered by physical proximity in real time. A child using the app is immediately visible on this radar to all nearby adult users — creating both a digital and a real-world proximity risk from the moment the app is opened.

👨‍👩‍👧 Section 3

What Parents Should Know

1
Mamba Is Designed Exclusively for Adults 18 and Older
Every feature on Mamba — from its matching algorithm and radar discovery to its video dating and virtual gifts system — is built around adult social and romantic life. The platform makes no provision for younger users and applies no content filtering to profiles, messages or live video within the app.
2
Video Dating Escalates the Risk Significantly Beyond Text Chat
Mamba's built-in video dating feature means that contact between a child and an unknown adult is not limited to text. Live video calls create real-time, face-to-face interaction between strangers — enabling far more intimate and potentially harmful contact than a messaging exchange alone, and creating a substantially higher risk of exploitation.
3
Curiosity and Regional Peer Influence Drive Installation
In the CIS region, Mamba is widely known among older teenagers and young adults. Children may install the app after encountering it through peers, older siblings or social media — particularly given its high profile in Russian-speaking markets. Its broad social reputation can make it seem more acceptable than apps marketed more explicitly as dating platforms.
4
Age Verification Can Be Bypassed in Seconds
Mamba requires only a date of birth and contact details to register. A child can create a complete adult account by entering a false birthdate — gaining immediate access to the full platform, including radar discovery, swipe matching, video calls and private messaging, without any further identity check.

💡 Section 6

Guidance for Parents

Three practical steps to protect your child and build lasting digital awareness.

💬
Discuss Online Relationships and Digital Safety With Children
Have open conversations about how location-based dating platforms like Mamba work — particularly that the radar feature shares their real-time physical location with nearby unknown adults, and that video calls enable live contact with strangers. Help children understand that a familiar app store listing does not make an adult platform safe, and that contact with unknown adults carries real-world risks regardless of the medium.
🤝
Encourage Communication Within Trusted Social Circles
Help your child build and invest in the friendships and family relationships they already have, rather than seeking connection with unknown adults through dating platforms. Children with strong, trusted real-world relationships are significantly better protected against the appeal of adult social discovery apps — regardless of how the platform is marketed or how widespread it is among peers.
🔒
Use Parental Controls to Limit Access to Adult-Focused Apps
Apply iLimits restrictions consistently across every device your child has access to — including tablets, spare phones and shared family devices. Review saved payment methods and in-app purchase settings to prevent coin purchases. Revisit your controls regularly as your child grows, and keep honest family conversations running alongside them for the most complete layer of protection.
