Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how iLimits – Parental Control & Screen Time App ("iLimits", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when parents or legal guardians use our application.

By using iLimits, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1

Who This App Is For

iLimits is designed exclusively for parents or legal guardians to manage and monitor their child's device usage. Children do not create independent accounts without parental involvement.

2

Information We Collect

Information provided by parents
  • ✓ Parent account email address
  • ✓ Child profile details (name or nickname – optional)
  • ✓ Screen time limits, app rules, and schedules
Automatically collected information
  • ✓ App usage duration and screen time statistics
  • ✓ List of installed apps (package names only, for categorization and limit enforcement)
  • ✓ Device identifiers (non-personal) for syncing parent and child devices
  • ✓ App configuration and enforcement settings
3

Accessibility Service Usage

🔒

iLimits uses Android Accessibility Service strictly to enforce parental controls set by you as a parent. This access is never used to monitor personal content or communications.

Accessibility access is used only to
  • ✓ Detect which app is currently active on the device
  • ✓ Apply app blocking and screen time limits
  • ✓ Prevent access to restricted apps as configured by parents
⚠️ Important Clarifications
  • ✗ We do NOT read, record, or transmit screen content
  • ✗ We do NOT capture keystrokes, passwords, or any text input
  • ✗ We do NOT access messages, calls, photos, contacts, or personal communications
  • ✗ Accessibility permissions are used solely for enforcing the parental controls you set
4

Child Data Protection

Protecting children's privacy is a core principle at iLimits. Every decision about data is made with children's safety and wellbeing in mind.

🛡️
No Ads
Zero advertisements are ever shown to children
🔐
Private Data
We never sell or share child data with third parties
👨‍👩‍👧
Parent Control
Data is only accessible by parents and guardians
5

How We Use Information

  • ✓ Enforce screen time and app usage limits as configured
  • ✓ Provide clear and accurate usage reports to parents
  • ✓ Sync settings across linked parent and child devices
  • ✓ Improve app stability, reliability, and performance
🚫

iLimits does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking. Ever.

6

Data Sharing

✋

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information or child data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data may only be disclosed in the following limited circumstances:

  • ✓ When legally required (e.g., valid court order or law enforcement request)
  • ✓ To protect the safety, rights, or security of iLimits, our users, or others
  • ✓ With service providers who help operate our app (e.g., cloud hosting), under strict confidentiality agreements
7

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely using encryption and industry-standard security practices. We use secure cloud infrastructure with restricted access controls. While no system is 100% secure, we continuously monitor and update our security measures.

🔐
End-to-End Encryption
All data encrypted in transit and at rest
🏢
Secure Infrastructure
Industry-standard cloud servers with restricted access
8

Data Retention & Deletion

  • ✓ Data is retained only while the parent account is active
  • ✓ Parents may request deletion of their account at any time
  • ✓ Upon account deletion, all associated child and parent data is permanently removed, except where retention is required by law
Account & Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their iLimits account and all associated personal data by contacting us at support@ilimits.app from their registered email address, or by following the account deletion instructions provided within the app.

ℹ️

Partial data deletion without deleting the account is not currently supported.

9

Permissions Used

  • ♿
    Accessibility Service
    Used exclusively to detect the active app and enforce screen time limits and app blocking rules set by parents.
  • 📊
    Usage Access (App Usage Stats)
    Required to calculate accurate screen time and generate usage reports visible to parents.
  • ⚙️
    Foreground Service
    Ensures parental controls remain active in the background and cannot be easily bypassed.
  • 🔐
    Device Admin (optional)
    Prevents uninstallation of the app without explicit parent permission.
10

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage parents to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your family's data.

11

Your Rights

As a parent or legal guardian, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • ✓ Access and review the data we collect about your child
  • ✓ Request correction of inaccurate information
  • ✓ Request deletion of your account and associated child data
  • ✓ Withdraw consent and stop using the app at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@ilimits.app
